HIII, IM FLOKKI!🧡

I'm an 18-year-old artist from Portugal who mostly draws Furry and Oc art.
My passion for drawing started when I was a kid, but I started focusing more on it
in about 2020, mainly during the quarantine, which resulted in me starting to post my
drawings on Twitter to share them and try to find some artist friends I could relate to
(Spoiler alert: I did :) ). My dream job is to be an artist or animator at a game company,
like Riot Games (I love Valorant and Arcane, what can I say), but I also have big dreams
of becoming a musician, I love music and I think it is such a powerful tool to unite people,
and how strongly it affects your humor, I mostly listen to rap, hip-hop, and rock but more
on the emotional side (I'm sensitive🥲). But yeah, that's me for now, if you want to
talk more you can use the form below this section to contact me.
(or use my socials, I'm more active there)
